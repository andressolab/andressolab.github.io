Project Overview

The goal was to create a new app for Auto Mercado to bring a different shopping experience for
their customers. Alongside the project, I created the bases for the design system that the team was using.

The challenge

The research of the project was conducted by Designit, so the main challenge was to
understand the research and create a design that reflects the insights found in it.

The process:

- Understanding the previous investigation.
- Creating low fidelity wireframes to test the app concepts.
- Designing high fidelity wireframes based on the brand’s identity.
- Creating high fidelity prototypes to test the app.
- Conducting interviews to iterate in the design process.
