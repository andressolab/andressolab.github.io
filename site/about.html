About Resume

“Collect moments, not things.”

I’m a proactive person, fast learner and hardworking with a desire to learn something new and live new experiences every day. I consider myself spontaneous, and look for every day to be worth it.

Currently I’m working in Costa Rica as a
Senior Service and Interaction designer at Accenture.

What do I believe in?

😉 Teamwork is the key.

We’re not alone in the world therefore we cannot work alone.
By communicating with our team, stakeholders, users,
anyone who’s involved in the process of creating a product,
the final result will be better.

🤓 Learn something new, and then, another thing.

Listen to what other people got to say, read a book or an
article, watch a movie, but keep learning something new
every day. I always try to challenge and improve myself to
keep growing everyday.

🎨 Be creative.

Creativity is like a muscle, we need to train it. When I’m not
designing sometimes I like to ilustrate, or to create stories
and characters for video games.

🙏 Stay humble.

Everyone’s got a voice, and ego is the real enemy. Treat
everyone with respect as we would like to be treated.

As a UX Designer , I have had worked on projects for clients both in Costa Rica and in the US (like Microsoft
among others) in which I am involved in the end-to-end process of UX projects. Some of my day-to-day tasks
are using tools such as user personas, scope canvas, heuristics, site maps, journey maps, architecture
information, wireframing, etc. Also, I do believe one can never know it all so I keep myself in constant learning
and exploring different fields to keep an open mind.

Also, in my career I became closer to the Service Design field in which I've learned and put it practice tools that
helped me understand the services and that also helped stakeholders understand their clients. For example
service blueprints, user research, journey maps, etc.

I also designed interactions to show in high-fidelity prototypes and developed both style guides and design systems .

For confidentiality reasons, some prototypes are not included in the portfolio, however, they can
be shown in an interview.

I also create video games 🎮 in my free time.

thanks for stopping by, let’s talk!
