How I do it?

Get to know the people. Always keep in mind the people we’re designing for. There’s no user experience with our users. User Experience. Approach design with honesty and positive intentions. Good design is inclusive, and for the people, not for the technology./n/nInterface Design. Create a design that’s both accessible, and beautiful. I believe in creating emotions thru design.

Why I do it?

The reason why I love working as a UX/UI designer is because I've got the possibility to create a connection between people and my work, help users with their problems and make a connection by exploring creative concepts and creating experiences that fit their needs.

I also create video games 🎮 and sometimes illustrations 🖍.
