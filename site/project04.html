Project Overview

Today there are many start ups, the question is, how do we differentiate one from the other? The
answer: providing a service that adds value to its customers. That is why with this client I seek to
transmit trust, security and commitment.

The problem?

Create a website from scratch, without the guidance of a brand or design system, that aligned
with the goals and vision of the company.

The process:

- Developing an information architecture of the full site sections.
- Creating low fidelity wireframes to test the site concepts.
- Designing high fidelity wireframes based in the brand’s identity.
- Creating high fidelity prototypes to test the site.
- Conducting user testing interviews.

The Solution:

A high quality site focused on people with the aim of clearly communicating how the company's
services work to promote transparency with its clients.
