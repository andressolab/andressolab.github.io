Project Overview

A couple years ago in my house we adopted a pet named Mia. There’s one thing I’ll never forget and it was
that my dad told me that the feeling of adopting is so nice. So I decided that as a personal project
I’ll make and app were people can put pets in adoption and adopt them too.

The problem?

After conducting a little reasearch, I realized that in Costa Rica, by the time, there isn’t an
trustworthy app to make adoptions.

The process:

- Researching the current status of the adoptions in Costa Rica.
- Creating user personas to understand the possible users.
- Creating the Architecture information of the site.
- Developing the wireframes of the app for both people who put in adoption and the ones who adopt.

The Solution:

After reviewing an app developed here in Costa Rica, and studying some others arround the globe,
I realized that the people main need was to adop a pet in a simple way, because of the long and
confusing processes that involve adopting.

And for the ones who rescue pets, the goal was to provide tham a platform were they can share
those pets and find them a home.
