Project Overview

Auto Mercado S.A. is one of the most important supermarket corporations in Costa Rica. Due to COVID19, they increased their sales thru e-commerce which result in the idea of re-designing the website to make it more accessible and attractive for their clients.

The problem?

The first version of the Auto Mercado site worked just fine, but users were experiencing usability problems and the site didn't reflect the brand's identity.

The process:

- Developing an information architecture of the full site sections.- Creating low fidelity wireframes to test the site concepts.
- Designing high fidelity wireframes based in the brand’s identity.
- Creating high fidelity prototypes to test the site.

The Solution:

After conducting an investigation analyzing the different e-commerces available in Costa Rica, the new Auto Mercado website was designed taking into consideration the best usability practices, as well as maintaining the freshness of the brand and the values ​​that it represents.

Likewise, to maintain consistency in the design, I worked on creating the basis of a design system for the team.
