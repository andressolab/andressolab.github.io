Project Overview

The goal was to create a site for the car company Ford in Costa Rica. I had to apply
the brand globally established among the years, and a consistency among the Ford
sites around the globe.

The challenge

The main challenge was to work for a brand with the exposition that Ford has around
the globe, because despite of this beign the Costa Rican site, it still belong to a group
of Ford Sites arround the globe.

The process:

- Looking for references for the site.
- Designing the first look and feel of the app to aprove with the client.
- Creating a style guide to maintain consistency along the site.
- Developing the mock ups of the app for both desktop and mobile.
