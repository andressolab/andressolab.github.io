Game Design Experience

This is a personal project in which I put into practice my creative skills for the
development of video games. Since I was little I have had a fascination for
video games, they are part of my daily life, that is why I have always been
curious about how they are created and the factors that influence the
experience of the people who play them.

Client

Personal Project

My Role

Art Developer and Game Designer / Team Manager

Time

+5 years

Project Overview

It was 2018, I was invited to participate in something called "Game Jam", I wasn't very sure
what it was, that day, something activated in me, video games stopped being a
hobby, they became a product to study.

For the last few years I have used my creative skills to generate art for video games
together with my best friends who are developers.

Also, I focused on studying game design, as well as the best practices to take a game
from an idea to a playable product.

The challenge

Learn about the process of creating old games from a human-centered approach.

In this project I have put into practice my creative, leadership and
communication skills to work with people from other disciplines as well as I
have investigated the video game creation industry to learn good practices
and team management.

The process:

- Learn about good practices to generate art for 2D and 3D video games.
- Learn about good practices on game design.
- Communicate assertively with the development team.
- Ideation and conceptualization of ideas for video games.
- Develop technical skills to learn about software to create video games.
- Participation in +4 national and international game jams.
- Coordination of a community to share knowledge about video game development. (2018-2020)
- Implementation of good teamwork practices.
- Study good UXUI practices focused on video games.
